BIOGRAPHY

My name is Elias Corbett and I am a professional drummer in the Ottawa, Ontario region. Drumming became a time passer and new hobby for me during Covid-19 lockdown times. I developed a passionate playing style for genres such as punk and heavy metal, however am not limited to. I would say from that my top five influences are: My Chemical Romance, Metallica, Korn, Napalm Death, and Queens of the Stone Age.
I have officially played with 3 bands since I started drums in March 2020.
